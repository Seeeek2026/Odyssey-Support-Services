What We Offer

Support tailored to you

We provide flexible, goal focused NDIS services that help you live well and build lasting independence.

Community Access

We support you to get out and take part in community life, including social events, recreation and everyday outings, helping you build connections that matter.

Capacity Building

Goal focused support to develop your skills, confidence and independence, from daily living tasks through to longer term personal growth.

Mentoring and Coaching

Dedicated one to one mentoring that encourages growth through physical challenges, outdoor experiences and building practical life skills.

Daily Living Support

Reliable assistance with routines, appointments and everyday tasks that help you stay on track and maintain your independence.

Sport and Active Recreation

From AFL and golf to gymnastics, the beach and outdoor adventures, we love being active and share many of the same passions as the people we support.

Genuine Connections

We prioritise building strong, meaningful relationships, taking the time to understand your unique needs, interests and goals so our support is truly person centred.

Why Odyssey

Support you can trust

We bring genuine passion, experience and professionalism to every interaction, because your journey deserves the very best.

  • Experienced and Qualified
    More than ten years of combined experience working with people of all abilities across a wide range of settings.

  • Fully Compliant
    Working With Children Checks, police checks, First Aid and CPR, NDIS clearances and public liability insurance are all in place.

  • Active and Community Focused
    We are active, outdoorsy and love getting out and about, which makes us a great fit for community engagement and an active lifestyle.

  • Flexible and Available
    Based across Adelaide's northern suburbs and happy to travel throughout the greater Adelaide region.
