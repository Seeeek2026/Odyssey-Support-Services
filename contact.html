Common Questions

Frequently asked

Who can access Odyssey Support Services? +

We support participants aged 10 and over under the NDIS. We work with young people and adults living with a wide range of disabilities across the greater Adelaide region.

Do I need an NDIS plan to access your services? +

Our services are funded through the NDIS. If you have an active plan that includes Community Participation or Capacity Building supports, you may be able to use our services. Please get in touch and we can help you understand your options.

Where do you provide support? +

We are based in Adelaide's northern suburbs and happy to travel across the greater Adelaide region. Please get in touch and we can discuss whether we are able to support you in your area.

How do I get started? +

Simply reach out by phone, email or the contact form above. We will arrange a relaxed, no obligation chat to learn about your goals and see whether we are a good fit for your journey.
