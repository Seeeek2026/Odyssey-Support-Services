Our Mission

Every journey is unique, and so is our support

At Odyssey Support Services we believe that finding the right support match is the foundation of success. We bring patience, energy and genuine care to every relationship, working alongside participants to celebrate progress, build skills and make every day meaningful.

I am goal oriented and dedicated, and I bring patience, energy and a genuine passion for helping people grow, whether that is through outdoor adventures in nature, getting involved in sport, or providing reliable assistance with daily tasks and routines that build independence.

I place real value on creating strong, meaningful connections that are tailored to each person's individual needs and aspirations. I am based in the northern suburbs and happy to travel, and I am here to be the support partner who helps you reach your goals, whatever your journey looks like.

BL
Director and Support Worker

Director at Odyssey Support Services

Brandon Luna

I am an Adelaide based independent support worker, and I helped start Odyssey Support Services to make a real difference in people's lives. I am enthusiastic and passionate about encouraging young people, from childhood through to adolescence, to get out and about and take on physical and mental challenges that show them just what they are capable of.

I have more than eight years of experience working with children of all abilities through Bounce Inc, including two years running dedicated disability groups. In that time I have seen how gently stepping outside your comfort zone, in a safe and supportive environment, builds genuine confidence and self belief. I also coach gymnastics and trampoline skills for children aged 5 to 15, which gives me a great toolkit for active and engaging support.

What Drives Us

Our values

Genuine Passion

We genuinely love what we do. Support is more than a job to us, it is a calling, and that enthusiasm comes through in every session, every outing and every goal achieved.

Respect and Dignity

We communicate openly, respect individual routines and always put the person first, listening to understand before we act.

Goal Focused

Every person has different dreams. We work alongside you to set meaningful, achievable goals and to celebrate each milestone along the way.

Let us find out if we are the right match

Finding the right support match is the key to success. Reach out and let us have a conversation about your goals and your journey.
